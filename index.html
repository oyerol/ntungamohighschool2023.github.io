Why I choose Ntungamo High School

We are thrilled to kick off another exciting academic year filled with endless possibilities and opportunities for growth. At Ntungamo High School, we believe in fostering a supportive learning environment that encourages curiosity, creativity, and collaboration.

Whether you're a returning student or new to our school community, we extend a warm welcome to you. Our dedicated team of educators is committed to providing an enriching and engaging educational experience for every student. Together, let's embark on a journey of discovery, learning, and success.

We encourage you to explore our website, get to know our staff, and stay updated on school events and activities. If you have any questions or need assistance, feel free to reach out to our friendly staff. Here's to a fantastic school year filled with achievement, camaraderie, and memorable moments!

Wishing you all the best,
